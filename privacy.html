Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

SmartQuote ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered quote generation service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company name
  • Password (encrypted)

2.2 Quote Data

When you generate quotes, we collect:

  • Trade/industry type
  • Job descriptions
  • Client information you provide
  • Generated quote content
  • Date and time of quote creation

2.3 Payment Information

Payment information is processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy governs the collection and use of payment information.

2.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Usage statistics (number of quotes generated, features used)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the SmartQuote service
  • Generate AI-powered quotes based on your input
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send you service-related emails (verification, password reset, receipts)
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For AI quote generation
  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and database
  • EmailJS: For sending emails
  • Netlify: For hosting

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS encryption
  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard algorithms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request account deletion at any time by contacting jpiccampbell@yahoo.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your quote data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

SmartQuote is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using SmartQuote, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the service.
