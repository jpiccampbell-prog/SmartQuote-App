Account Deletion Request

How to Delete Your SmartQuote Account

To request deletion of your SmartQuote account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to: jpiccampbell@yahoo.com
  2. Subject line: "SmartQuote Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address in the email body
  4. We will process your request within 48 hours

Quick Email Template:

To: jpiccampbell@yahoo.com
Subject: SmartQuote Account Deletion Request
Body: "Please delete my SmartQuote account associated with [your email address]"

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account information (name, email, company name)
  • All saved quotes and quote history
  • Authentication credentials
  • Usage statistics and preferences
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data Retention

Payment Records: If you have subscribed to SmartQuote Pro, payment transaction records will be retained for 7 years as required by tax and financial regulations. This includes:

  • Transaction IDs and dates
  • Payment amounts
  • Billing information (processed by Stripe)

All other personal data will be deleted within 48 hours of your request.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or data privacy, please contact us at jpiccampbell@yahoo.com
